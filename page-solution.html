What We Provide

1

合规运营全托管

Full-Stack Compliance Management

我们了解各州和城市的具体法规要求，根据需要完善 Haochi!Collective 平台的整体合规经营体系，同时协助合作摊主完善个人经营合规资格，让摊主安心经营，不必担心任何检查与罚单。

We understand state and city regulations inside and out. We build and maintain Haochi!Collective's complete compliance framework, while helping every co-vendor secure their personal operating credentials — so you can focus on cooking, not paperwork.

2

全美活动进驻

Nationwide Event Placement

我们以合作摊主工作地点为目标，负责调研和筛选优质的在地美食节市集活动，并负责全过程申报进驻，让摊主省心出摊，并不断提升每年有效出摊日，持续经营。

We scout and select premium local food festivals and markets near you, handle the full application and placement process — so you show up, cook, and grow. We steadily increase your effective event days each year for long-term, sustainable operations.

3

专业培训体系

Professional Training System

提供线上+线下双轨并行的培训服务体系，涵盖出品制作、服务标准、食品安全、合规经营、销售技巧等全方位出摊技能，让新手摊主快速上手，经营绩效持续提升。

Our dual-track training — online and offline — covers food preparation, service standards, food safety, compliance, and sales techniques. New vendors ramp up fast; experienced ones keep improving.

4

供应链运营支持

Supply Chain & Operations Support

从原料采购、仓储物流到收银系统、一体化移动餐厨设备升级，我们正在极力地完善海外餐饮出摊的基础设施和服务，致力于降低摊主运营成本，提升移动餐饮摊位部署和撤离效率，减少人工消耗，让持续摆摊经营变得简单易行。

From ingredient sourcing and logistics to POS systems and modular mobile kitchen equipment, we're building the infrastructure that makes overseas food vending seamless. Lower costs, faster setup and teardown, less labor — so you can keep doing what you love.
