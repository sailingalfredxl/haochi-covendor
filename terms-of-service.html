1. 服务描述

1. Description of Services

Haochi!Collective 为在美华人美食摊主提供以下服务：

Haochi!Collective provides the following services to Chinese food vendors in the US:

  • 合作摊主孵化：为有意向的华人美食从业者提供品牌孵化、产品开发和市场推广支持
  • 活动资源对接：对接美食节、市集、主题活动、音乐节等北美活动资源
  • 产品供应链支持：整合美国本地食材和包材供应链资源
  • 品牌营销赋能：提供社交媒体营销、数据分析等品牌增长工具
  • Vendor incubation: providing brand development, product sourcing, and marketing support for aspiring Chinese food entrepreneurs
  • Event access: connecting vendors with food festivals, markets, themed events, and music festivals across North America
  • Supply chain support: integrating US-based ingredient and packaging supply chain resources
  • Brand growth enablement: providing social media marketing, data analytics, and brand growth tools
