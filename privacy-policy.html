Privacy Policy

HAO CHI COLLECTIVE INC. 致力于保护您的个人信息安全

Committed to protecting your personal information

生效日期：2026年1月1日 Effective Date: January 1, 2026

HAO CHI COLLECTIVE INC.（以下简称"我们"或"Haochi!Collective"）承诺尊重并保护您的隐私。本隐私政策（以下简称"政策"）解释了当您访问我们的网站 www.haochicollective.com（以下简称"网站"）、使用我们的服务、或与我们合作成为合作摊主（以下统称"服务"）时，我们如何收集、使用、披露和保护您的个人信息。

HAO CHI COLLECTIVE INC. ("we," "us," or "Haochi!Collective") is committed to respecting and protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy ("Policy") explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you visit our website www.haochicollective.com ("Website"), use our services, or partner with us as a vendor ("Services").

1. 我们收集的信息

1. Information We Collect

1.1 您主动提供的信息

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • 基本信息：姓名、电话号码、电子邮件地址、邮寄地址
  • 身份验证信息：政府签发的身份证件（如驾照）、社会保险号（仅在需要时）
  • 业务信息：摊位名称、产品类别、餐饮许可/执照文件、商业保险证明
  • 申请材料：通过网站表单提交的所有内容，包括但不限于介绍、图片、食谱、品牌故事
  • 沟通记录：您与我们之间通过电子邮件、电话或网站表单进行的通信内容
  • Basic contact information: name, phone number, email address, mailing address
  • Identity verification: government-issued ID (e.g., driver's license), Social Security Number (SSN only when required)
  • Business information: booth name, product categories, food handler permits/licenses, proof of commercial liability insurance
  • Application materials: all content submitted through our website forms, including but not limited to introductions, photos, recipes, and brand stories
  • Communication records: correspondence with us via email, phone, or website forms

1.2 自动收集的信息

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • 设备信息：浏览器类型、操作系统、唯一设备标识符（UDID）
  • 使用数据：访问的页面、停留时长、点击行为、引荐来源、搜索查询
  • 位置信息：IP地址推断的大致地理位置（城市/州级别）
  • Device information: browser type, operating system, unique device identifiers (UDID)
  • Usage data: pages visited, time on site, click behavior, referral source, search queries
  • Location information: approximate geographic location inferred from IP address (city/state level)

1.3 第三方来源信息

1.3 Information from Third Parties

在适用法律允许的范围内，我们可能从以下来源获取关于您的信息：活动策展方、社交媒体平台（如您通过社交账号登录或关注我们的社交媒体）、信用报告机构（用于核实合作摊主的信用状况）。

To the extent permitted by applicable law, we may obtain information about you from: event organizers, social media platforms (if you log in via social accounts or follow our social media), and credit reporting agencies (to verify vendor creditworthiness).

2. 我们如何使用您的信息

2. How We Use Your Information

  • 服务提供：处理合作摊主申请、安排活动档期、进行资质审核
  • 沟通联络：发送与活动相关的通知、回复咨询、提供客户服务
  • 服务改进：分析网站使用行为，改善用户体验和平台功能
  • 营销推广：经您同意后，发送活动预告、优惠信息、新闻通讯（您可随时退订）
  • 法律合规：履行法律义务、响应政府或监管机构的合法要求
  • 安全防护：检测和预防欺诈、滥用或安全威胁
  • 商业交易：若发生企业并购、资产出售或重组，我们可能在相关交易中转移您的信息
  • Service delivery: processing vendor applications, scheduling event slots, conducting qualification reviews
  • Communication: sending event-related notifications, responding to inquiries, providing customer support
  • Service improvement: analyzing website usage to enhance user experience and platform features
  • Marketing: with your consent, sending event previews, promotional offers, and newsletters (you may unsubscribe at any time)
  • Legal compliance: fulfilling legal obligations, responding to lawful requests from government or regulatory authorities
  • Security: detecting and preventing fraud, misuse, or security threats
  • Business transactions: in the event of a merger, acquisition, asset sale, or reorganization, we may transfer your information as part of the transaction

3. 信息共享与披露

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

我们不会出售您的个人信息。以下情况除外：

We do not sell your personal information. We may disclose your information in the following circumstances:

  • 服务合作方：活动策展方、场地供应商、支付服务商、物流配送方（仅在提供服务所必需的范围内）
  • 法律要求：响应法院命令、传票、执法机构请求，或遵守适用法律
  • 保护权利：当我们真诚地认为披露信息对于保护我们的权利、保护您或他人的安全、调查欺诈或响应紧急情况必要时
  • 业务转让：在企业合并、收购或资产出售中
  • Service partners: event organizers, venue providers, payment processors, and logistics providers (only to the extent necessary to provide services)
  • Legal requirements: in response to court orders, subpoenas, law enforcement requests, or to comply with applicable law
  • Protecting rights: when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, the safety of you or others, investigate fraud, or respond to an emergency
  • Business transfers: in connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale

4. Cookie 与追踪技术

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

我们使用 Cookie 和类似的追踪技术来提升您的网站体验：

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your website experience:

  • 必要 Cookie：确保网站基本功能正常运行，如语言偏好设置、会话管理
  • 分析 Cookie：了解访客如何与网站互动（使用 Google Analytics 等工具）
  • 功能 Cookie：记住您的偏好设置（如中英文切换）
  • 营销 Cookie：经您同意后，用于跨网站广告投放
  • Necessary cookies: required for basic website functionality such as language preferences and session management
  • Analytics cookies: to understand how visitors interact with the site (using tools like Google Analytics)
  • Functionality cookies: to remember your preferences (e.g., language settings)
  • Marketing cookies: with your consent, for cross-site advertising

大多数浏览器允许您阻止或删除 Cookie。如需了解如何管理 Cookie，请参阅浏览器帮助文档。

Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. To learn how to manage cookies, please refer to your browser's help documentation.

5. 数据安全

5. Data Security

我们采取合理的技术和管理措施来保护您的个人信息免遭未经授权的访问、使用、披露、篡改或销毁。这些措施包括：数据加密（SSL/TLS 传输加密）、访问控制（限制员工访问权限）、定期安全评估、以及安全的服务器存储环境。

We implement reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, use, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include: data encryption (SSL/TLS in transit), access controls (limiting employee access on a need-to-know basis), regular security assessments, and secure server storage environments.

重要提示：任何互联网传输或电子存储方式都无法保证100%安全。虽然我们竭尽全力保护您的信息，但我们无法保证其绝对安全。

Important: No method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. 您的权利

6. Your Rights

6.1 通用权利

6.1 General Rights

根据适用法律，您可能享有以下权利：

Depending on applicable law, you may have the following rights:

  • 知情权：了解我们收集了哪些关于您的信息以及如何使用
  • 访问权：请求获取我们持有的关于您的个人信息副本
  • 更正权：请求更正不准确或不完整的个人信息
  • 删除权：请求删除您的个人信息（受某些例外情况限制）
  • 限制处理权：请求在特定情况下限制我们处理您的信息
  • 数据可携权：请求以结构化、机器可读的格式接收您的信息
  • 拒绝权：拒绝我们基于合法利益处理您的信息
  • Right to know: to understand what personal information we collect about you and how it is used
  • Right to access: to request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Right to correction: to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information
  • Right to deletion: to request deletion of your personal information (subject to certain exceptions)
  • Right to restrict processing: to request that we limit processing of your information in specific circumstances
  • Right to data portability: to receive your information in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to object: to object to our processing of your information based on legitimate interests

6.2 加州居民权利（CCPA/CPRA）

6.2 California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

如果您是加利福尼亚州居民，根据《加利福尼亚消费者隐私法》（CCPA）和《加利福尼亚隐私权法》（CPRA），您享有额外的权利，包括：不受歧视的权利、要求披露我们收集、使用和披露的个人信息的权利、以及要求删除您的个人信息的权利。如需提交请求，请联系下方提供的联系方式。

If you are a California resident, under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), you have additional rights including: the right not to be discriminated against, the right to request disclosure of personal information we collect, use, and disclose, and the right to request deletion of your personal information. To submit a request, please contact us using the information provided below.

6.3 纽约居民权利（NY SHIELD Act）

6.3 New York Residents (NY SHIELD Act)

如果您是纽约州居民，根据《纽约 Stop Hacks and Improve Electronic Data Security Act》（SHIELD Act），您有权在发生涉及您个人信息的数据安全事件时收到通知。

If you are a New York resident, under the New York Stop Hacks and Improve Electronic Data Security Act (SHIELD Act), you have the right to receive notification in the event of a data security incident involving your personal information.

7. Third-Party Links

我们的网站可能包含指向第三方网站、服务或应用程序的链接（如活动合作伙伴网站、社交媒体平台）。我们不对这些第三方的隐私惯例负责。建议您在访问任何第三方网站时查阅其隐私政策。

Our website may contain links to third-party websites, services, or applications (such as event partner websites and social media platforms). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies when visiting any third-party sites.

8. 儿童隐私

8. Children's Privacy

我们的网站和服务不面向13岁以下儿童（美国）或16岁以下儿童（欧盟/英国）。我们不会故意收集13岁以下儿童的个人信息。如果您认为我们收集了未成年人的信息，请立即联系我们，我们将立即删除相关数据。

Our website and services are not directed to children under 13 years of age (US) or 16 years of age (EU/UK). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under these ages. If you believe we have collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately and we will promptly delete the relevant data.

9. 政策变更

9. Changes to This Policy

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。任何重大变更将在网站上发布更新版本，并注明新的生效日期。在适用法律要求的范围内，我们将在重大变更生效前通过电子邮件或网站公告通知您。建议您定期查阅本政策以了解最新内容。

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any material changes will be posted on the website with a revised effective date. To the extent required by applicable law, we will notify you by email or through a notice on our website before any material changes take effect. We encourage you to review this Policy periodically for the latest information.
