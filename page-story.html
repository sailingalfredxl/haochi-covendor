关于我们 About Us

推动地道中式街头美食走向世界

Bringing Authentic Chinese Street Food to the World

支持赋能海外华人美食小微创业可持续发展

Empowering Overseas Chinese Food Entrepreneurs to Build Sustainable Micro-Businesses

那不只是一张煎饼——那是手艺，是一方水土，是几代人的清晨。
That wasn't just a breakfast wrap. It was craft. It was culture. It was generations of people starting their day the same way.

我从小在中国南京老城最具烟火气的街巷里长大。凌晨五点，街角支起的煎饼摊已经飘出第一缕油烟——葱花在铁板上滋滋作响，香气穿过三条街。那是我认识的第一种生活方式。

I grew up in the alleyways of Xi'an. By five in the morning, the jianbing stall on the corner was already alive — the sizzle of scallions drifting three streets over. That was the first way of life I ever knew.

移民美国后，我在法拉盛看到了另一面：有手艺、有家传食谱、有梦想的华人——被困在每天重复的生活里。不是不想做，是不知道怎么开始，没有门路，没有圈子。

In Flushing, I saw another reality: talented Chinese immigrants with incredible family recipes and big dreams — stuck in the daily grind. Not unwilling to start, just without a path, a network, or a way in.

"这是奶奶做的味道。"
"This tastes like my grandmother's cooking."

2023年，我们支起第一个摊位，带来了生煎包、肉夹馍。队伍排到街角，美国人要加单，第三代移民端着餐盒说出这句话。那一刻，我知道了我们要做的事。

In 2023, we set up our first stall. The line wrapped around the block. Americans asked for seconds. A third-generation immigrant said this holding their container. In that moment, we knew exactly what we were meant to build.

街头美食从来不只是食物。它是一种语言——关于家，关于传承，关于人情味。我们想把这种语言带向更远的地方，赋能每一个怀抱手艺的你，把故事变成可以持续经营的生意。
Street food was never just food. It's a language of home, heritage, and human connection. We want to take that language further — and empower you to turn your story into a sustainable business.

今天，Haochi!Collective 的每一位成员，不只是在出摊——我们是一家人，让风味连接，让美食变现。

Today, every member of Haochi!Collective isn't just selling food — we're family. Connect through flavor. Monetize your craft.
